Business Activity Restrictions
Condition Business Reduction Required Business Expansion Prohibited
Net Capital Ratio < 125% of minimum < 150% of minimum
AI/NC Ratio > 12:1 > 10:1
Alternative Method < 4% of debits < 5% of debits
15 Business Day Rule: Conditions must exist for more than 15 consecutive business days before FINRA imposes requirements.
Broker-Dealer Solvency
Must Cease Business and Notify Regulators If:
  • Placed in voluntary/involuntary bankruptcy
  • Trustee/receiver appointed
  • General assignment for creditors' benefit
  • Admission of insolvency
  • Unable to compute Net Capital or Customer Protection Rule compliance
Expense Sharing Requirements
Requirements to Exclude from Net Capital:
  1. Written agreement with parent company
  2. Third party written agreement of non-liability
  3. No indication of broker-dealer liability
  4. Not a GAAP liability of broker-dealer
  5. Third party has adequate independent resources
Documentation Requirements:
  • Record ALL expense sharing agreements
  • Maintain allocation records
  • File notice with FINRA including agreement date and parties
  • Provide agreement copy to DEA upon request
