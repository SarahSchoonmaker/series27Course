3. Special Loan Types
Temporary Subordinated Loan:
  • Maximum 3 per year
  • 45 days maximum duration
  • Requires AI/NC < 10:1
  • Net Capital > 120% minimum
Revolving Subordinated Loan:
  • Prepayment allowed under 1-year minimum
  • Requires FINRA approval
  • Net Capital must exceed 200%
  • AI/NC must stay below 9:1
4. Withdrawal and Prepayment Restrictions
Cannot Withdraw/Prepay If:
  • AI/NC would exceed 10:1
  • Net Capital would fall below 120% minimum
  • Alternative Method: Net Capital < 5% of debits
  • SEC may restrict withdrawals for 20 business days
Early Warning Conditions:
  • Net Capital falls below 120% minimum
  • AI/NC ratio exceeds 12:1
  • Alternative Method: Net Capital below 5% of debits
5. Special Provisions
Owner/Partner Loans:
  • 3+ year term with 1+ year remaining treated as equity
  • Minimum 12-month duration
  • No recourse to firm assets
  • FINRA approval required
Bank Parent Provisions:
  • Must use third-party bank
  • No parent bank certificates
  • Parent bank deposits are non-allowable
  • Must be used for business operations
Customer vs. Non-Customer Classification:
  • Unapproved customer loans count as AI
  • Unapproved non-customer loans (partners, parent company) not AI
  • Approved loans never count as AI
