Types of Fully Disclosed Broker-Dealers

Receives Securities Broker-Dealer

Net Capital Requirement: $50,000
  • ✓ Can receive customer securities
  • ✓ Uses clearing firm agreement
  • ✓ Can join best efforts underwritings
  • ✕ Cannot hold customer funds/securities
  • ✕ Cannot be syndicate member ($250k required)

Non-Receiving Broker-Dealer

Net Capital Requirement: $5,000
  • ✕ Cannot receive customer securities
  • ✓ Uses clearing firm agreement
  • ✓ Can join best efforts underwritings
  • ✕ Cannot join firm commitment underwritings
  • ✕ Cannot hold customer funds/securities
