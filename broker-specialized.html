Specialized Broker-Dealer Types

Prime Broker
$1,500,000 Min. Net Capital (Clearing)

Capital Requirements

  • Clearing Prime Broker: $1,500,000 minimum
  • Executing Broker: $1,000,000 minimum
  • Must notify FINRA of prime broker status

Services Provided

  • Execute all trades
  • Settle all trades
  • Maintain custody
  • Provide financing
Primarily used by hedge funds for consolidated clearing and financing
Market Maker
$100,000 Base Min. Net Capital

Capital Requirements

  • $1,000 per security bid ≤ $5
  • $2,500 per security bid > $5
  • Maximum requirement: $1,000,000
Example Calculation:
20 positions × $1,000 = $20,000
20 positions × $2,500 = $50,000
Total Required: $70,000

Position Requirements

  • 25% of market value for long positions
  • 30% of market value for short positions
  • Early warning at 120% of requirement
Mutual Fund Broker-Dealer
$5,000 - $25,000 Net Capital

Capital Requirements

  • $25,000 if accepting wire orders
  • $5,000 if subscription-only orders

Operating Conditions

  • All orders through sponsor
  • No customer fund holding
  • Wire redemption within 1-3 days
Higher capital for wire orders due to temporary fund advances
Reverse Repo Requirements
Additional 10% Capital Required

Excess Collateral Thresholds

  • Treasury securities: 105%
  • Agency/mortgage-backed: 110%
  • Other securities: 120%

Capital Requirements

  • 10% of excess collateral over thresholds
  • Based on market value of securities
  • Calculated per agreement type
Prevents misuse of excess collateral
