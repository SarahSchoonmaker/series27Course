Net Capital Requirements & Related Rules

Alternative Net Capital Computation

Standard "AI" Method

The firm's ratio of Aggregate Indebtedness to Net Capital must be computed under this method.

This is the default method unless firm qualifies for Alternative Method

Alternative Method Requirements

Minimum capital must be greater of:

• $250,000

• 2% of total debits in Reserve Formula

Under Alternative Method:
  • No AI computation required
  • Most items are customer-related liabilities
  • Often used by large retail firms with excess capital
  • Helps avoid technical violations of AI/NC ratio requirements
Currency Position Haircuts

Major Foreign Currencies

6% haircut applies to:

• Euro

• British Pound

• Swiss Franc

• Canadian Dollar

• Japanese Yen

Other Currencies

20% haircut applies to all other foreign currencies

Only applies to non-offset positions
Gold & Silver Requirements
Condition Requirement
Control Must be in BD's control with serial number identification and immediate disposition ability
Form Must be in good deliverable form (refined/assayed, acceptable to exchanges)
Insurance Must be insured at all times (in custody, depository, or transit)
Commodities & Futures Rules

Partner/Owner Accounts

Any equity in commodities accounts cannot be used to increase Net Worth

Receivables

• Management fees from pools: Allowable for 30 days

• Clearing corporation receivables: No time limit
