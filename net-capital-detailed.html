Asset Classifications for Net Capital

Allowable

Cash and Cash Equivalents

- Cash: Immediately available for claims

- Cash in Special Reserve: Required for customer protection

ⓘ
Allowable

Customer Debit Balances

- Secured by customer securities

- Collateral can be liquidated if needed

ⓘ
Allowable

Fails to Deliver

- Recent settlement failures

- Expected to be resolved shortly

ⓘ
Allowable with Haircut

Inventory

- Securities held for trading

- Subject to 15% haircut for equities

ⓘ
Non-allowable

Prepaid Expenses

- Rent, insurance advances

- Uncertain recovery in liquidation

ⓘ
Non-allowable

Exchange Membership

- Trading rights and seats

- Value uncertain in liquidation

Step 1: Calculate Total Available Capital

Net Worth $400,000
+ Subordinated Loans $200,000
Total Available Capital $600,000

Step 2: Deduct Non-allowable Assets

Prepaid Expenses $50,000
Exchange Membership $25,000
Real Estate $225,000
Total Non-allowable Assets $300,000

Step 3: Calculate Haircuts

Inventory Market Value $100,000
Equity Haircut (15%) $15,000

Final Net Capital

Total Available Capital $600,000
- Non-allowable Assets $300,000
- Haircuts $15,000
Final Net Capital $285,000
